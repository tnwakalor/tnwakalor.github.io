WEBSITE EVALUATIONS

Bob Saget

This is a website that showcases information about Bob Saget, an american stand-up comedian. The site advertises one of his recently published books for purchase and gives us news about his most recent tour

  • Contrast: The relevant content does not stand out really. The Location of certain elements like the menu is awful.
  • Repetition: There's barely any repetition with the layouts, fonts, sizing of elements etc.
  • Alignment: This is where this website fails the most. The alignment with most of the elements are terrible. Super inconsistent. Makes the site look disorganized.
  • Proximity: Related elements are barely grouped together as it is hard to identify what content he was trying to display.

Daniel Aristizabal

This is a portfolio website for a graphic designer. The creator made well organized website to showcase his graphic design work

  • Contrast: Although the color scheme is simple. The relevant content still stands out due to the consistent font all-around and proportionate sizing in the middle of the page as you scroll up and down.
  • Repetition: Repetition is clearly visible on this website. The creator did a good job with being consistent with the colors,fonts and features.
  • Alignment: There is good alignment witht the content and the menu and the links on the four corners of the website.
  • Proximity: This is attributed perfectly in this website as related elements are all grouped together. It was easy to identify the content he was trying to show to us visitoors.
